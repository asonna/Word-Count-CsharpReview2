#Specifications


* _Behavior1: Check a word again same word
* _Input: WORD, WORD
* _Output: WORD
* _Description: To ensure that the word can be located

* _behavior2: Check a word again itself and validate a count of 1
* _Input: WORD, WORD
* _Output: 1
* _Description: To first insure that our match is register as 1

* _behavior3: Check a word again string of words with a single match and still return a count of 1
* _Input: LIFE IS NOT EASY, LIFE
* _Output: 1
* _Description: To first insure that our match is located within a string and still return a count of 1

* _behavior4: Check a word again string of words with multiple match and return number of matches
* _Input: A DOG OWNER TOOK HIS DOG INTO A DOG STORE, DOG
* _Output: 3
* _Description: To ensure that the recurrences of matches are accounted for

* _behavior5: Check a word again string of words with word appearing as partial in other words
* _Input: A CAT WAS CATCHING A NAP AT THE CATHEDRAL, CAT
* _Output: 1
* _Description: To ensure that partial words or embedded words are not been counted

* _behavior6: Check a word again string of words with no match
* _Input: I WILL ACE THIS REVIEW, REALLY
* _Output: "Your original word has no match to your string; Please try another combination."
* _Description: To ensure that no matching scenario are recognized by the program
